FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What are your hours of operation?

We're open every day from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM, 365 days a year, so you can drop off, pick up, or schedule service whenever it works best for you.

Do you provide pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer free pickup and delivery for all of our standard services. Simply schedule online or by phone and we'll handle the rest.

How does wash & fold pricing work?

We charge $2.25 per pound with a 30 lb minimum for standard pickup & delivery. For 24-hour turnaround, the rate is $2.50 per pound. No hidden fees.

What detergents do you use?

We use premium, eco-friendly detergents that are gentle on your clothes and the planet. We can accommodate sensitivities — just let us know your preferences when scheduling.

Do you handle commercial accounts?

Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes including hospitality, medical, restaurants, gyms, salons, AirBnBs, and more. Contact us for a custom consultation and pricing.
